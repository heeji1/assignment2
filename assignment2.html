The critical conservation areas issues

Forest

Terrains are changing due to human activity, primarily in forestry and agricultural production.

Currently, one-third of all land is either degraded or in the process of degrading, endangering vital ecosystem services like carbon storage and harming biodiversity.

Protected zones provide an answer. Such places can assist sustainable development if they are equitably and successfully administered.

They can also preserve human health and well-being, preserve natural resources and cultural assets, and offer sustainable livelihoods.
